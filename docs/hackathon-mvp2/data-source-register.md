# Data Source Register

This register catalogues the datasets and references evaluated for research and model development within the HealthGuard AI V2 platform.

| Dataset | Owner | Population | Study design | Individual or aggregate | Available predictors | Available target | Access type | Licence status | Suitable HealthGuard task | Unsuitable task | Lifecycle status |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **ICMR-INDIAB current sample** | ICMR | Representative metabolic risk sample across 15 states in India | Cross-sectional epidemiological survey | Individual-level anonymised research sample | Age, gender, height, weight, BMI, waist circumference, physical activity, dietary patterns, BP, glucose | Diabetes classification (via HbA1c / oral glucose tolerance) | Restricted academic request | Research-use only | Pre-laboratory screening prioritisation (research validation) | User-facing real-time clinical diagnostics | `VALIDATION_CANDIDATE` |
| **ICMR YDR** | ICMR | Individuals diagnosed with youth-onset diabetes in India | Disease registry | Individual-level registry database | Clinical diagnosis data, age of onset, family history, insulin levels, complications | Type of diabetes, clinical outcomes | Academic application | Research-use only | Researching youth-onset diabetes profiles and longitudinal complications | General diabetes-versus-no-diabetes screening (unsuitable because it is a registry of people already diagnosed with youth-onset diabetes) | `RESEARCH_ONLY` |
| **NSS Health 2025** | MoSPI | National-level health consumption in India | Multi-stage stratified household survey | Household and individual aggregate tables | Household income, healthcare spending, morbidity, treatment details, hospitalisation | Health status, out-of-pocket expenditure | Public domain / MoSPI portal | Open Government Licence (OGL) | General demographic health expenditure analysis, resource planning | Individual-level clinical risk score calculation | `RESEARCH_ONLY` |
| **NSS 75** | MoSPI | Household social consumption on health (2017-2018) | Cross-sectional stratified household survey | Individual-level microdata | Chronic illness presence, hospitalisation rates, immunisation, demographics | Self-reported chronic illness (diabetes/hypertension) | Public domain / MoSPI portal | Open Government Licence (OGL) | Socio-economic health risk profiling, geographic prevalence analysis | High-precision biomarker prediction, real-time physiological risk evaluation | `RESEARCH_ONLY` |
| **Census 2011** | ORGI | All Indian citizens | National census | Aggregate tables (district/state level) | Gender, age, literacy, occupation, rural/urban density | None (no disease biomarkers) | Public domain | Public domain | Geographic normalisation of demographic inputs and baseline weights | Biological disease screening or individual metabolic risk evaluation | `RESEARCH_ONLY` |
| **NCRP 2020** | NCDIR-ICMR | Cancer patients in network hospitals in India | Clinical registry | Aggregate reports and patient registries | Age, gender, cancer site, clinical stage, treatment modality, tobacco habits | Cancer diagnosis, survival rate | Restricted academic request | Research-use only | Epidemiology trend analysis, public health cancer burden studies | Metabolic risk screening (diabetes/cardiovascular/hypertension) | `RESEARCH_ONLY` |
| **HMIS API** | MoHFW | Facility-level indicators in public clinics | Administrative health records | Facility-level aggregate indicators | Institutional births, immunisations, outpatient attendance, screening volume | Clinic-level service delivery metrics | Open API / MoHFW portal | Open Government Licence (OGL) | Clinical resource planning and regional clinic density lookup | Patient-level screening risk calculation or clinical assessment | `RESEARCH_ONLY` |
| **NFHS aggregate API** | IIPS & MoHFW | District/State-level aggregate health and nutrition indicators (NFHS-4/5) | Demographic and health survey (DHS) | Aggregate indicator values | Prevalence of anaemia, obesity, hypertension, high blood sugar, infant mortality | Regional prevalence statistics | Public API | Open public access | Region-level baseline probability adjustment for metabolic risk factors | Patient-level clinical risk score calculation | `RESEARCH_ONLY` |
| **Pending NFHS-5/LASI/SAGE requests** | IIPS, WHO & MoHFW | Individual-level survey records for household health, aging (LASI), global health (SAGE) | Longitudinal household and clinical cohort studies | Individual-level microdata | Cognitive function, metabolic biomarkers, height, weight, BP, dry blood spot assays, socio-economic factors | Diabetes, cardiovascular conditions, cognitive decline | Under request approval process | Pending research agreements | Longitudinal risk projection modeling, geriatric cognitive-metabolic study | Direct production deployment (until agreement and validation are completed) | `RESEARCH_ONLY` |
